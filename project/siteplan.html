Community Sports Court Booking and Management – Site Plan

Site Name

Community Sports Court Booking and Management

This name clearly reflects the purpose of the site: to help community members organize and reserve the use of available sports courts, such as soccer fields or basketball courts. We will use the domain playlocal.org; it's a warm, short name that encourages participation.

Site Purpose

This website is designed to allow users to:

  • View existing reservations for community sports courts.
  • Make new online reservations for games or events.
  • Check a calendar of upcoming sporting events or recreational activities.
This will help avoid scheduling conflicts and promote better use of the facilities, encouraging community participation.

Scenarios

  • How can I check if the soccer field is available for Saturday afternoon?
  • Where can I see the upcoming community sports events scheduled for this month?
  • Can I reserve a basketball court for a private event next weekend, and how will I receive confirmation?

Color Scheme

These colors convey both professionalism and freshness, helping to create a pleasant and organized visual experience:



Primary: #617C4B – Used for buttons, call-to-action, highlights.
Secondary: #1E281D – Used for secondary buttons, navigation hovers.
Background: #f9fbfc – Used as the page background color.
Heading Text: #0F79B2 – Used for titles and main headings.
Body Text: #1E281D – Used for paragraphs and general text.
Accent: #3B6E23 – Used for links, hover states, small UI elements.

Typography

  • Headings: 'Oswald', sans-serif – A strong, modern font suitable for sports branding.
  • Body text: 'Roboto', sans-serif – Clean and highly readable, ideal for paragraphs and longer text.
