“El Pulgarcito de América”

El Salvador, known as the “El Pulgarcito de América”, is a small country full of culture, breathtaking landscapes and warm people.

Salvadoran gastronomy stands out for its authentic flavor, with pupusas as the main dish, accompanied by riguas, tamales, fried yucca and mountain coffee. Eating in El Salvador is an experience of flavor.
